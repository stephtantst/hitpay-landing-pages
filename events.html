Frequently asked questions

Everything wedding planners and event management companies need to know about HitPay.

How can wedding planners collect milestone payments professionally?

HitPay Invoicing supports multi-stage milestone invoicing. You define each payment stage — for example, 30% booking deposit, 40% mid-project milestone, and 30% final balance — with individual due dates. HitPay sends a professional invoice and automatic reminder for each milestone. Clients pay with a single click via PayNow, card, or GrabPay.

Can event planners collect deposits via WhatsApp to lock in a client?

Yes. With HitPay Payment Links, you create a deposit link in under 60 seconds — with the deposit amount, event description, and your logo — and send it via WhatsApp. Once the client pays, you receive instant confirmation. This locks in their commitment before any planning work begins, and eliminates the back-and-forth of chasing bank transfer confirmation screenshots.

Can I sell event tickets with different pricing tiers — Early Bird, Standard, VIP?

Yes. HitPay's Online Store lets you list multiple ticket types with different prices, quantities, and descriptions on a single event page. You can set Early Bird limits that auto-close when sold out, collect attendee information at checkout (name, dietary requirements, T-shirt size), and track sales in real time — without paying a third-party ticketing platform fee.

How do I set up monthly retainer billing for a corporate event client?

HitPay's Recurring Billing lets you create a monthly retainer plan for each corporate client. The client authorises the first payment and HitPay charges them automatically every month — sending a receipt ideal for their finance team to process as an expense. You can manage multiple corporate retainer clients from the same dashboard.

What payment methods can wedding and event clients use?

HitPay supports PayNow, Visa/Mastercard, GrabPay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Atome BNPL for splitting large deposits interest-free. Corporate clients can also pay via direct bank transfer through the invoice. All payment methods are tracked in a unified dashboard so you always know exactly what has been paid and what remains outstanding.

My wedding clients keep delaying deposit payments — how does HitPay help me collect faster?

HitPay Payment Links turn a multi-step collection process (share bank details → client transfers → you check your bank → you confirm) into one step. Send a WhatsApp message with your deposit link and the client pays in 30 seconds from their phone. You receive an instant notification the moment payment lands. Faster collection means you lock in bookings before clients shop around with other planners — especially important in the competitive wedding planning market.

Can I issue partial or full refunds for event deposits through HitPay?

Yes. HitPay's dashboard lets you initiate full or partial refunds against any past transaction. For example, if your policy allows a 50% refund for cancellations more than 60 days before the event, you can refund exactly that amount with a few clicks. Card refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days; PayNow refunds are usually instant. Your refund policy is entirely your own — HitPay simply processes what you decide.

Can I accept walk-in registrations and on-the-day payments at my event?

Yes. HitPay's card terminal or Soundbox handles on-site registration desk payments — accepting card tap, PayNow, and GrabPay. You can also generate a one-time payment link on your phone for last-minute walk-ins who want to pay on the spot without a card. All on-the-day payments sync to your event dashboard in real time alongside pre-paid ticket sales, giving you a complete revenue picture.

I offer wedding packages at different price tiers — can I create separate deposit links for each?

Yes. You can create and save multiple payment links — one per package tier — each with its own amount, label, and description. For example: an "Essential Package Deposit — S$3,000" link and a "Prestige Package Deposit — S$6,000" link. Each link is reusable for future clients of that tier and all deposits are tracked together in your dashboard, giving you a clear view of confirmed bookings by package type.

Is HitPay suitable for a freelance wedding coordinator who is not a registered company?

Yes. HitPay accepts sole proprietors and individual freelancers — you don't need a Pte Ltd company. You can sign up with your personal NRIC and a personal bank account, and start accepting professional invoices and payment links within 1–2 business days. This makes HitPay ideal for freelance wedding coordinators, independent MCs, and photographers who need a professional payment collection solution without the overhead of incorporating a company.
