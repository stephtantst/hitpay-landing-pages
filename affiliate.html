The Model

0.1% of TPV. Every month. No cap, no expiry.

The commission structure is intentionally simple. For every dollar your referred merchants process through HitPay — cards, PayNow, GrabPay, or any of HitPay's 50+ payment methods — you earn 0.1% per month. There are no tiers, no minimum thresholds to hit before you start earning, and no expiry window on referred merchants. As your clients' businesses grow, your passive income grows with them. A merchant that starts at S$200K/month and scales to S$2M/month takes your commission from S$200 to S$2,000 — automatically, without any action on your part.

Frequently asked questions

Everything developer and agency partners need to know about the HitPay Affiliate Program.

How much can a partner earn from the HitPay Affiliate Program?

A partner earns 0.1% of the total payment volume processed by each linked merchant per month. For example, a merchant processing S$1,000,000 per month generates S$1,000 in monthly commission. There is no cap on the number of merchants or total commission amount. Partners with five merchants averaging S$500,000 per month each would earn approximately S$2,500 per month — S$30,000 annually.

When are commissions paid?

Commissions are calculated automatically on the first of each month for the previous calendar month's transaction volume. The calculated amount is credited directly to the partner's HitPay wallet and flows out via HitPay's standard payout transfer. No manual claim, invoice, or request is required from the partner.

How does a partner track their affiliate earnings?

Partners have access to a dedicated commission dashboard that shows a paginated record of every commission payout — by merchant and by month. A one-click CSV export is available, covering Business Name, Email, Amount, Currency, and billing period. Per-merchant breakdowns are available to see exactly how much each client contributed in any given period.

Is there a minimum transaction volume required to start earning?

No. There is no minimum transaction volume required to begin earning affiliate commissions. Commission accrues from the very first successful transaction processed by a linked merchant. The 0.1% rate applies to the full monthly volume once the partner account is in accepted status and at least one merchant is linked.

How long does a partner continue earning commission from a referred merchant?

There is no expiry window on affiliate commissions. As long as the linked merchant continues processing payments through HitPay and the partner account remains in good standing, commissions continue to accrue and be paid monthly. A merchant linked today will continue generating commission indefinitely — without any renewal or re-referral required.

What transactions are included in the commission calculation?

All succeeded charges processed by linked merchants are included — cards, PayNow, GrabPay, ShopeePay, and all other supported HitPay payment methods. Cash transactions are excluded from the commission calculation. Commissions are currently calculated in the partner's wallet currency; cross-currency merchant volume from merchants operating in a different currency will be added in a future update.

Can the Affiliate Program be combined with the Reseller or Platform Partner programs?

Yes. The affiliate commission programme is designed to stack with — not replace — HitPay's Reseller Programme (Malaysia) and Platform Partner Program. Partners operating across multiple tiers earn commissions under each applicable programme simultaneously. A single HitPay partner account can hold all three programme designations at once.

How does a partner get started with the HitPay Affiliate Program?

Partners apply through their HitPay dashboard. After approval, they can begin linking merchants — both new referrals and existing clients already on HitPay — to their partner account. Once merchants are linked and begin processing, commissions accrue automatically from the first transaction in that calendar month.

How long does affiliate partner approval take?

Partner applications are typically reviewed and approved within 1–3 business days. The process is fully online and does not require any upfront fees, minimum revenue commitment, or hardware purchase. Once approved, the partner can immediately begin linking merchants and earning commission.

Can a partner add existing HitPay merchants to their affiliate account?

Yes. Merchants that are already active on HitPay — including clients the partner onboarded before joining the affiliate programme — can be linked retroactively once the partner account is approved. This means partners who have been building HitPay integrations for clients can begin earning commission on existing transaction volume immediately after approval.
