Frequently asked questions

Everything gyms, studios, and fitness businesses need to know about HitPay.

How can gyms and fitness studios automate monthly membership billing?

With HitPay's Recurring Billing, you create your membership plans — for example, S$88/month for Basic Access — and share the sign-up link with new members. They subscribe once and HitPay automatically charges them on their renewal date each month, sends a receipt, and retries any failed payments. You see all active members, MRR, and upcoming renewals in one dashboard.

What happens if a member's monthly payment fails?

HitPay automatically retries failed payments using smart dunning logic — attempting the charge again at optimal intervals to maximise collection rate without manual effort. Members receive an automated notification to update their payment method. You can see all failed attempts, retries, and outcomes in your dashboard without any manual follow-up.

Can I sell drop-in class passes and trial sessions via WhatsApp or Instagram?

Yes. HitPay Payment Links let you create a class-specific link in under 60 seconds and share it via WhatsApp, Instagram Stories, or link-in-bio. Clients pay to lock in their spot and you receive instant confirmation. You can set a quantity limit on each link to prevent overbooking — the link automatically closes when all spots are filled.

Can members manage or cancel their own gym membership?

Yes. HitPay provides a member self-service portal where members can update their payment method, view billing history, pause, or cancel their membership — without needing to call or WhatsApp you. This reduces admin time significantly and improves member experience, particularly for members who travel or take seasonal breaks.

Is HitPay suitable for small yoga, pilates, or CrossFit studios with under 50 members?

Absolutely. HitPay has no monthly platform fees and no minimum member count — studios with 10 members or 500 members pay the same low per-transaction rate. This makes HitPay accessible from day one, even for studios that are just getting started. Many boutique yoga and pilates studios use HitPay as their primary billing system.

I'm currently collecting gym fees manually via PayNow every month — how do I automate this?

Set up HitPay Recurring Billing once: create your membership plan, send each member their personal subscription sign-up link, and they authorise the first payment. Every subsequent month, HitPay charges them automatically and sends a receipt — you don't touch anything. Most gym owners complete the migration of all members within a week. After that, you never manually check your bank app for gym fee transfers again.

Can I offer new members a free trial period or discounted first month before charging full price?

Yes. HitPay supports trial periods and introductory pricing. You can create a separate "First Month" plan at a reduced rate and then migrate the member to your standard plan after the trial. Alternatively, set up a plan with a discounted first charge and standard recurring amount from the second charge onward. This is a common strategy for CrossFit boxes and yoga studios running "first week free" or "first month at 50%" promotions.

Does HitPay integrate with Mindbody or Glofox gym management software?

HitPay connects to Mindbody, Glofox, and other gym management platforms via Zapier — you can set up automations where a new member in your booking system triggers a HitPay subscription link being sent to them automatically. For deeper integrations, HitPay's REST API allows custom connections to any gym management system. Contact the HitPay sales team for enterprise integration support and implementation guidance.

Can I sell personal training packages and supplement bundles from the same HitPay account?

Yes. Your HitPay account handles both recurring memberships and one-off product sales from the same dashboard. Your Online Store can list PT session packages, 10-class bundles, protein supplements, branded apparel, and accessories side by side. Customers can purchase any product in a single checkout, and all revenue — whether from memberships or merchandise — is tracked in one unified report.

How long does it take to set up HitPay and migrate existing gym members to auto-billing?

Account setup typically takes 1–2 business days for verification. Once approved, you create your membership plans in under 10 minutes and send each existing member a subscription sign-up link via WhatsApp. Most members complete the sign-up in under 2 minutes. For a gym with 100 members, the full migration can realistically be completed within a week — and often much faster if you bulk-send links with a simple WhatsApp broadcast message.
