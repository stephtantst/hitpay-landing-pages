AUTOMATIC PAYOUTS

No invoicing. Your wallet. Every first of the month.

Once your referred business goes live and processes their first online payment on HitPay, commissions start accruing. Your wallet balance reflects the sum of all active referrals — which means a single great referral compounds quietly in the background while you run your own business.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the HitPay Refer and Earn programme work?

Any existing HitPay merchant can refer other businesses using a unique referral link found in their dashboard under Refer and Earn. When the referred business registers through that link, goes live, and starts processing online payments, the referring merchant earns 0.1% of the referred business's monthly online payment volume — credited automatically to their HitPay wallet on the first of each month.

How much can a merchant earn from referring businesses to HitPay?

A merchant earns 0.1% of the total online payment volume processed by each business they refer, every month. A referred business processing S$300,000 per month in online payments generates S$300 monthly for the referrer. There is no cap on earnings and no limit on the number of businesses that can be referred.

Where does a HitPay merchant find their referral link?

The referral link is available inside the HitPay merchant dashboard. Log in, then click Refer and Earn in the left navigation menu. The unique referral link appears there and can be copied or shared directly. No application or approval is required — the link is available to all active HitPay merchants.

When are referral commissions paid?

Referral commissions are calculated automatically on the first of each month for the previous calendar month's online transaction volume. The amount is credited directly to the referring merchant's HitPay wallet with no manual claim, invoice, or request required.

What types of transactions are included in the commission calculation?

Commission applies to online payment transactions only — cards, PayNow, GrabPay, ShopeePay, payment links, and online checkout processed through HitPay. POS/terminal transactions and recurring billing transactions are excluded from the commission calculation.

Is there a limit on how many businesses a merchant can refer?

No. There is no limit on the number of businesses a HitPay merchant can refer. Every business that registers through the referral link and goes live on HitPay contributes to the referring merchant's monthly commission — there is no cap on the total earnings.

How long does a merchant continue earning commission from a referral?

There is no expiry on referral commissions. As long as the referred business continues processing online payments through HitPay, the referring merchant continues to earn 0.1% of their monthly online payment volume every month — with no renewal, re-referral, or action required.

Does the referred business need to be a new HitPay customer?

The referred business must register through the referral link. Businesses that are already existing HitPay merchants would not count as a new referral. The programme is designed for businesses that have not yet signed up with HitPay — share the link with any business owner looking for a payment solution.
