Common questions from vendors

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Can international vendors from Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, or China use HitPay at the fair?
Yes. International vendors accept PayNow via HitPay's Borderless QR — no Singapore bank account or local business registration required. Singapore shoppers pay in SGD; HitPay automatically converts and pays out to your home bank. Vendors from Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Japan, the Philippines, and more are all eligible.
Do I need to buy or rent a card terminal to accept card payments?
No terminal needed for Singapore vendors. Tap to Pay on any NFC iPhone or Android phone — customers tap their card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay directly on your device. If you prefer a dedicated reader, HitPay card terminals are available, but they're optional.
How long does HitPay account approval take?
Approval typically takes 1–3 business days after document submission. Sign up as early as possible using the partner link to ensure your account is live and tested before the fair. Documents required: proof of business registration in your home country, and your personal ID.
When do I receive my payout after the fair?
Singapore vendors receive PayNow payouts in T+1 calendar day, and card payouts in T+3 working days — directly to their Singapore bank account. International vendors receive payouts in T+3 working days in their home currency. No manual action required on your end.
Is there any monthly fee or minimum commitment?
No setup fee, no monthly fee, no contract. HitPay charges only a small percentage per transaction: 2.5% for domestic cards, 0.4% for PayNow (Singapore vendors), or 1.5% for Borderless QR (international vendors). Nothing to pay when the fair ends.
What payment methods can shoppers use at the fair?
Shoppers can pay by Visa, Mastercard, PayNow, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. No cash counting, no wrong change, no missed sales from "I don't have cash".
