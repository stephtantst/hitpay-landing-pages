Pre-Consultation Payments

Collect deposits before appointments to cut no-shows and late cancellations

Clinic no-shows cost revenue and block appointment slots that could serve other patients. HitPay's payment link feature lets reception staff send a consultation deposit request directly to the patient's phone — via WhatsApp, SMS, or email — before the appointment is confirmed. Patients in Singapore pay via PayNow, Malaysian patients via DuitNow or Touch 'n Go, and Philippine patients via GCash, Maya, or InstaPay. The payment link requires no patient account or app installation, and the clinic sees confirmation in the HitPay dashboard in real time. A financial commitment at booking consistently reduces non-attendance, and deposits are automatically applied to the final invoice at checkout.

  • Send deposit requests via WhatsApp, SMS, or email in under 30 seconds

  • Set deposit amounts per appointment type — specialist consults, dental procedures, aesthetic sessions

BNPL & Instalment Payments

Remove cost as a barrier to treatment with buy now, pay later for larger procedures

Dental implants, orthodontic treatment, specialist diagnostic packages, and aesthetic procedures often carry price tags that cause patients to defer care or pay in ways that create cash flow friction for the clinic. HitPay integrates Atome and ShopBack PayLater for clinics in both Singapore and Malaysia, and Grab PayLater for Malaysian clinics, allowing patients to split treatment costs into 3 monthly instalments with zero interest to the patient. Critically, the clinic receives the full treatment amount upfront from the BNPL provider — there is no revenue deferral or collection risk on the clinic's side. This arrangement is particularly valuable for dental practices in Singapore's Toa Payoh HDB hub and for specialist wellness clinics serving working professionals in Kuala Lumpur's KLCC corridor, where out-of-pocket healthcare spending is high.

  • Atome & ShopBack PayLater available in Singapore and Malaysia — 3-month split billing
  • Clinic receives full payment upfront — no collection risk or revenue deferral
  • Patients approve instalment plan in under 60 seconds on their phone at checkout
  • Works for any treatment type — dental, orthopaedic, aesthetic, or specialist consultation packages

Everything else your clinic needs to manage payments

From multi-counter reception setups to corporate billing and patient membership programmes — HitPay covers the full payment lifecycle.

Tap to Pay on iPhone

Accept Visa, Mastercard, and contactless payments directly on an iPhone — no card terminal hardware purchase required. Ideal for mobile clinic staff, home visit doctors, and satellite consultation rooms.

Automated digital receipts

Every transaction triggers an automatic digital receipt sent to the patient's email or phone. Receipts include itemised treatment details, GST/SST/VAT breakdowns, and clinic branding — removing the need for manual receipt printing.

Recurring wellness memberships

Subscription billing supports monthly or annual wellness packages, physiotherapy series, or TCM treatment plans. Patients' cards are charged automatically on the billing date, and failed payments trigger automated retry — reducing revenue leakage from lapsed memberships.

Corporate and insurance invoicing

Issue itemised invoices directly to corporate HR teams or insurance panel coordinators with an embedded payment link. Payment status tracking in the HitPay dashboard eliminates the need for follow-up phone calls or manual statement reconciliation with panel administrators.

PCI DSS compliant infrastructure

HitPay is PCI DSS compliant and licensed by MAS (PS20200643). No card data is stored on clinic devices or servers. This removes the compliance burden from clinic IT staff and protects patient payment information to the highest industry standard across all three markets.

Multi-staff and multi-counter access

Clinic managers assign role-based access to reception staff, billing officers, and practice managers. Each team member sees only the data relevant to their role. Transaction logs are attributed per staff member, supporting audits and reducing payment disputes in multi-doctor clinic environments.
