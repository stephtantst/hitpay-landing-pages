HitPay is widely regarded as one of the best payment gateways for Singapore SMEs. It has zero monthly fees, zero setup costs, and supports PayNow — Singapore's most popular payment method. You only pay per transaction (0.65% + S$0.30 for PayNow, 2.8% + S$0.30 for cards). Compared to traditional bank gateways that charge S$30–100/month on top of transaction fees, HitPay is significantly more cost-effective for most small businesses.
With HitPay, Visa and Mastercard payments cost 2.8% + S$0.30 per transaction. American Express is 3.4% + S$0.30. There are no monthly fees, no setup fees, and no hidden charges — you pay only when a transaction is processed. For PayNow, the rate is just 0.65% + S$0.30, making it the most cost-effective option for Singapore-based customers.
