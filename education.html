Frequently asked questions

Everything enrichment centres, tutors, and education businesses need to know about HitPay.

How can enrichment centres automate monthly tuition fee collection?

HitPay's Recurring Billing lets you set up monthly subscription plans for each student. Parents enter their card details once — or pay via GrabPay or GIRO — and HitPay automatically charges them on the renewal date every month, sending a receipt instantly. No manual invoicing, no chasing payments, no spreadsheets.

Can parents pay tuition fees via PayNow or GrabPay?

Yes. HitPay supports PayNow, GrabPay, Visa/Mastercard, Apple Pay, Atome, and bank transfers. Parents choose their preferred method at checkout. PayNow is especially popular in Singapore due to its zero-friction mobile experience, and its 0.4% rate is also the lowest available for digital payments.

How do I send payment reminders to parents for unpaid tuition invoices?

HitPay Invoicing includes automated payment reminders. Set a due date on each invoice and HitPay automatically sends email reminders before and after the due date — without any manual follow-up from you. This reduces awkward conversations with parents while improving your collection rate.

Can I sell trial class slots and term enrolment packages online?

Yes. HitPay's Online Store lets you create a product catalogue with trial classes, term enrolment bundles, workbooks, and digital downloads. You get a branded storefront at hitpay.me/yourschool that parents can access from their phones — no app, no website developer required.

Is HitPay suitable for small tuition centres or solo tutors?

Absolutely. HitPay has no monthly fees and no minimum transaction volume — making it ideal for solo tutors and small enrichment centres from day one. You only pay a small per-transaction fee when you collect a payment. Many one-person tuition businesses use HitPay's free Payment Links via WhatsApp to collect fees without any setup cost.

I currently collect tuition fees by bank transfer — what's the benefit of switching to HitPay?

Bank transfers are manual and hard to track — you check your account each month, match transfers to students, and follow up on missing payments yourself. HitPay automates this entirely: parents are charged automatically on their renewal date, receipts are sent instantly, and your dashboard shows in real time who has paid and who hasn't. Most centres that switch report saving 3–5 hours of admin work per month.

What is the cheapest way to collect monthly tuition fees in Singapore?

PayNow via HitPay is the most cost-effective method at just 0.4% per transaction — for a S$400/month tuition fee, that's only S$1.60. HitPay supports PayNow recurring billing, so parents can authorise an auto-debit once and be charged monthly without re-scanning a QR each time. There are no monthly platform fees, making HitPay one of the lowest-cost collection solutions available for tuition centres in Singapore.

Can I offer sibling discounts or different pricing tiers for different student groups?

Yes. HitPay lets you create multiple subscription plans at different price points — so you can set up a "Sibling Rate" plan at a discounted price alongside your standard plan, and enrol qualifying families accordingly. You can also apply one-time discounts on individual invoices before sending them to parents. There's no limit to the number of plans or pricing tiers you can create.

Is it safe for parents to store their credit card details for recurring tuition billing?

Yes. HitPay is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security, the same standard used by Visa and Mastercard themselves. Card details are tokenised by the card networks and never stored on HitPay's or your servers in raw form. Parents' payment information is protected to the same standard used by major banks and global e-commerce platforms like Amazon.

What documents do I need to sign up for HitPay as a tuition centre or enrichment school?

For Singapore-registered businesses, you'll need your ACRA business registration, the NRIC of directors, and a business bank account. For sole proprietors and individual tutors, your personal NRIC and personal bank account are sufficient — you don't need a company. The sign-up process is fully online and typically takes 1–2 business days for verification before you can start collecting payments.
